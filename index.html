  <h1>OFA - Account Deletion Policy</h1>


  <p><strong>Last Updated:</strong> July 2025</p>


  <h2>Overview</h2>

  <p>At <strong>OFA (One For All)</strong>, we respect your privacy and data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, we’ve made it simple for you to make that request.</p>


  <h2>How to Request Account Deletion</h2>

  <p>You can request account deletion by sending an email to:</p>

  <p><a href="mailto:help.oneforall.sup@gmail.com">help.oneforall.sup@gmail.com</a></p>

  <p>Include the email address associated with your OFA account in your message. Once verified, your data and account will be permanently deleted from our systems within 7 days.</p>


  <h2>What Will Be Deleted</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>All personal data associated with your OFA account</li>

    <li>Attendance records, timetables, preferences, and progress data</li>

    <li>Any feedback or activity linked to your account</li>

  </ul>


  <h2>What Will Be Retained</h2>

  <p>We do not retain any of your personal data after account deletion. Anonymous, aggregate statistics (which are not personally identifiable) may be retained for analytics and improvement purposes.</p>


  <h2>Need Help?</h2>

  <p>If you have any issues or questions, please reach out to us at: <a href="mailto:help.oneforall.sup@gmail.com">help.oneforall.sup@gmail.com</a></p>


  <p>Thank you for using OFA – One For All.</p>
