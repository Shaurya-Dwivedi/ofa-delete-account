OFA - Account Deletion Policy

Last Updated: July 2025

Overview

At OFA (One For All), we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated information, we have made the process straightforward for you.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account, please send an email to:

help.oneforall.sup@gmail.com

Please ensure you send the email from the address associated with your OFA account. Upon successful verification, your account and all related data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 7 days.

What Will Be Deleted

  • All personal data associated with your OFA account
  • Attendance records, timetables, and user preferences
  • All progress data and activity linked to your account
  • Any feedback you have provided through your account

What We Retain

We do not retain any of your personal data after your account is deleted. We may retain anonymous, aggregated statistics for analytical purposes to improve our services. This data is not personally identifiable.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or encounter any issues with the account deletion process, please do not hesitate to contact us at: help.oneforall.sup@gmail.com.
